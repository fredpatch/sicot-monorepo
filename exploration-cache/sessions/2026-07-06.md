## Found for free (not hunted for)

`DataTable` computes `colSpan` from `columns.length` automatically. This
incidentally fixes a pre-existing bug on `AuditPage.tsx`/`DocumentsPage.tsx`
where the empty/loading row's `colSpan` was hand-written and had drifted
out of sync with the actual column count — not something anyone was
looking for, just a side effect of `DataTable`'s design not having that
failure mode.

## Scope decisions (documented in `docs/TASKS.md`)

- **No generic `DataTable` column-filter prop surface.** Each page keeps
  its own bespoke filter bar (search/select combinations vary too much
  page to page) rather than trying to generalize filtering into the shared
  component.
- **AnalyticsPage's 6 small static tables use the plain `Table` primitives,
  not `DataTable`.** They have no pagination and no per-row actions beyond
  a button — `DataTable` (manual sorting/pagination/filtering) is reserved
  for tables with real server-driven state.
