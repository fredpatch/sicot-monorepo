The application would be used to mainly monitor:

Agreement lifecycle : From the moment they have been signed to the end, they want to be able to remind authorities or DG that hey we have signed an agreement with x organisation, x country, what of it, it is about to expire, or remind agreement between a certain party,
Partner contact list,
Agent missions repport and recommandation : In order for the CCIT to follow through, if they get information on the agent mission, location and etc, they can be behind the departmeent that is supposed to book the flight of the agents, so they dont get their ticket too late, or their mission funds too late too, and they can even contact the organisation where the agent is supposed to go on mission if needed to plan pick up at the airport or whatever, if there are recommendation at the end of the agent mission, they wannt to be able to monitor the implementation, so at a time, they can remind the user that they need to do something about x recommendation,
Correspondance : courriers that have been sent or received from organisation, or partners, and some expecting a reply, or not, or just a notice that the courrier was well received. cause CCIT noticed that sometimes there are letters that have been waiting for reply for more than 2 months, and that is not ok, so now they want to store a copy of such in the application, to monitor them, and reminds DG or the concerned party in the agency that hey, we received something some time ago, did you reply yet ??
Traductions : this is the hearth of the CCIT, this where they handle traduction, revision or archives . they are helped with the ai and should always review the traduction themself,
Demande de traduction : this module is a public one in the organisations, other department may request documents to be translated here, assign priorities (which is reviewed by admin), they can upload the document from there
Glossaire : it is to train the ai to aviation civile wordings, and should act as dictionary saving previous keywords, or accurate translation;
main document management section, using ocr to scan doc in order to ease the document lookup and traduction later too, this section aim to make all document available to internal agency users later, before and after translation, I think the ones marked archives are the one that should be available, and later for external user, we should have a portal for target consultation of docs defined by admin,
Administration should encapsulate the settings of the app, and main changes that can be made on some variables, or constant or rules,
Journal daudit: as its name is precise, this section should hold these specific information.
